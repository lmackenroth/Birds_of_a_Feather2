Birds of a Feather

Click in the image to create your birds!

Birds of a Feather is my Final Project for my Postmodern Design Class that highlights a generative art piece primarily coded using TypeScript.

My overall goal of this project was to create an abstract sky scene while incorporating user input. The "birds" are made up of minimalistic shapes, generated around the user's mouse click that then randomizes shape and color. The background cycles through ten random gradients, and every time the user refreshes the page, they will be different.
